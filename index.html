About Me

 
 

I've been playing games since my childhood, and from there, my love for games grew each year. I used to dream of what it would be like to work in a big gaming industry. When I was in school, I used to download different game engines and tools and play around with them, even if I had no idea what I was doing. Fortunately, I convinced my parents that I wanted to become a game developer, and I joined a university that had a B.Sc. degree in game development. From there, I published my first game on the Play Store, which I made in Unity with my friend. When I develop games, I love that every day I learn something new in game development.
